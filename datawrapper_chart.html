Syracuse University, Fall 2025

My story is about house inspections in the city of Syracuse, and how community members are impacted. In Syracuse, house inspections are done by the Code Enforcement agency, with numerous department working under the agency to perform the inspections. For my data visualization, I wanted to highlight which departments in Syracuse are leading the house inspections, and which department is able to resolve/close to most cases regarding to housing violations.
